My Place: Bidar

Bidar is a historic city located in the northeastern part of Karnataka, India. Known for its rich heritage, Bidar is famous for its magnificent fort, ancient monuments, and unique Bidriware handicrafts. The city has a vibrant culture and is home to several educational institutions. Bidar Fort, with its impressive architecture and scenic views, is a major attraction. The city is also known for its pleasant climate and friendly people, making it a wonderful place to live and visit.
